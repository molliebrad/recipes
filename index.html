Buffalo Chicken Dip

This stuff is incredible! So good.

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
1 Cup Canned Chicken
2 Tbsp Buffalo Sauce
8 oz Cream Cheese
2 Cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Instructions

  1. Mix everything together
  2. Place in 13X9 pan
  3. Bake at 350 degrees
  4. Enjoy!
